Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 15, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Hexa Dijital ("we", "our") provides digital marketing and social media management services. When you authorize our application via Pinterest OAuth, we may access your basic profile information, board details, and public Pins through Pinterest's official API.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used strictly for:

  • Authenticating your account via Pinterest Login.
  • Creating, scheduling, or managing Pins and boards on your behalf based on your explicit instructions within our platform.
  • Analyzing Pin performance and engagement metrics to improve your marketing strategy.

3. Data Security & Retention

We do not store your Pinterest password. Access is granted through secure, encrypted OAuth tokens. We do not sell or share your personal data with third-party advertisers. Data is kept only as long as necessary to provide our services.

4. User Rights & Revocation

You can revoke Hexa Dijital's access at any time through your Pinterest account settings (Apps section) or by contacting us directly. Revoking access will immediately stop our ability to interact with your Pinterest account.
